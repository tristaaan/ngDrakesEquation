The Drake Equation

R {{R}}: stars formed per year in our galaxy.
fp {{fp}}: fraction of the stars with planets.
ne {{n}}: the average number of planets per star that can potentially support life.
fl {{fl}}: fraction of the planets that develop life.
fi {{fi}}: fraction of planets that develop intelligent life.
fc {{fc}}: fraction of civilizations that develop a technology that emits signals through space.
L {{L}}: length of time in Earth years for which these civilizations emit detectable signals.
